What's VIX Digital ?

Meet us

We are a full service digital agency specialising in providing digital services to the public sector with teams in Warrington, London, Leeds and Sheffield. Our clients include NHS Digital, NHS England and Public Health England.

From complex CMS websites to bespoke product development, we work with our clients to build software that solves problems. Combining research, service design, software development and architecture, we work with clients throughout the lifecycle of their project from discovery through to building the live service and ongoing support.

Our multidisciplinary team spans designers to developers to information architects all with experience delivering projects in both the public and private sector. We use this experience to offer consultation services to help you create the right digital strategy for your team and service, as well as choosing the right technology for the job.
